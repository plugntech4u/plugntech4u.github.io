Redmi Note 15 5G Launched in India: Price, Specifications & Key Highlights

Posted on January 2026 | PlugNTech

Xiaomi has officially launched the Redmi Note 15 5G in India, expanding its popular Redmi Note lineup. The Redmi Note series is known for offering strong specifications at competitive prices, and the latest model continues this tradition.

Here is a clear overview of the Redmi Note 15 5G, including its price, specifications, and key highlights for Indian consumers.

Redmi Note 15 5G Price in India

The Redmi Note 15 5G is priced starting at ₹15,999 in India. The final price may vary depending on storage variants and bank offers available at the time of purchase.

Redmi Note 15 5G Specifications

  • Display: 6.6-inch Full HD+ Curved AMOLED display with 120Hz refresh rate
  • Processor: MediaTek Dimensity 7-series / Snapdragon 6-series chipset
  • Rear Camera: 50MP primary camera with AI enhancements
  • Front Camera: 16MP selfie camera
  • Battery: 5000mAh battery with fast charging support
  • Software: Android 15-based HyperOS
  • Connectivity: 5G, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, USB Type-C

Key Highlights

  • Smooth AMOLED display with high refresh rate
  • Reliable performance for daily use and gaming
  • Large battery suitable for all-day usage
  • Competitive pricing in the mid-budget 5G segment

Should You Consider Buying It?

The Redmi Note 15 5G is a solid choice for users looking for a balanced smartphone with 5G support, good display quality, and dependable battery life.

Final Verdict

With the Redmi Note 15 5G, Xiaomi continues to focus on value-driven smartphones for the Indian market. Buyers looking for a feature-rich 5G phone at a reasonable price may find this device worth considering.
