Redmi Note 15 5G Launched in India: Price, Specifications & Key Highlights

Posted on January 2026 | PlugNTech

Xiaomi has officially launched the Redmi Note 15 5G in India, strengthening its popular Redmi Note lineup. Known for delivering premium features at competitive prices, the Redmi Note series continues its legacy with a sleek design, powerful hardware, and long-term software support.

Here is a detailed overview of the Redmi Note 15 5G, including its key specifications, features, and why it stands out in the mid-range 5G smartphone segment.

Redmi Note 15 5G Price in India

Xiaomi has positioned the Redmi Note 15 5G as a value-focused mid-range smartphone. Pricing starts around the competitive mid-budget segment, with final prices varying based on storage variants and bank or festive offers available at the time of purchase.

Redmi Note 15 5G Specifications

  • Display: 6.7-inch curved AMOLED display with 120Hz refresh rate and up to 3200 nits peak brightness
  • Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 6 Gen 3 chipset
  • Rear Camera: 108MP MasterPixel camera with Optical Image Stabilization (OIS)
  • Video Recording: Shake-free 4K video recording support
  • Battery: 5520mAh EV-grade battery with 45W fast charging
  • Software: Android 15-based Xiaomi HyperOS
  • Audio: Dolby Atmos-enabled stereo speakers
  • Durability: IP66 rating, military-grade durability, Diamond Structure Protection
  • Connectivity: 5G, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, USB Type-C

Design & Display Highlights

  • Ultra-slim 7.35mm body for a premium in-hand feel
  • Curved AMOLED panel for immersive viewing
  • Excellent outdoor visibility even under harsh sunlight

Performance & Battery

Powered by the Snapdragon 6 Gen 3 processor and paired with 8GB RAM and 128GB storage, the Redmi Note 15 5G delivers smooth day-to-day performance. The large 5520mAh battery ensures long usage hours, while 45W fast charging allows quick top-ups.

Camera Capabilities

The 108MP OIS camera captures detailed photos in both daylight and low-light conditions. Optical Image Stabilization helps reduce blur, while 4K video recording delivers stable and professional-looking footage.

Software & Long-Term Support

The smartphone runs on Android 15 with Xiaomi HyperOS. Xiaomi has confirmed long-term support with up to 4 years of Android updates and 6 years of security updates, making the device future-ready.

Should You Consider Buying It?

The Redmi Note 15 5G is a strong option for users seeking a premium display, dependable performance, a high-resolution camera, and long-lasting battery life in the mid-range 5G category.

Final Verdict

With its bright AMOLED display, powerful Snapdragon chipset, durable build, and extended software support, the Redmi Note 15 5G emerges as a well-rounded smartphone for Indian consumers looking for long-term value.
